history

Lumine and Aether’s upbringing was a complicated one - more often than not denied of their individuality and constantly traveling around, never making roots anywhere. The only thing that never changed for her was Aether and she was also the only thing that never changed for them. So, obviously, when Lumine started seeing specters, it was no surprise that Aether followed. Maybe it was the infamous twin connection, but soon enough it was confirmed to both of them that no one was crazy - Amber showed up soon enough in a flurry of flames.
After that, it was like if the sands of time started running and they were introduced to the guardian world and ended up separating due to Lumine’s natural gift for Geo. She was taken in by the adepti, training under the watchful eye of the Vigilant Yaksha, while living in the Wangshen Funeral Parlour’s backroom, courtesy of Hu Tao. Nowadays, she tries to balance her training, her OF and her streams along with visiting Aether from time to time.

Lumine usually keeps her hair chopped at shoulder length, sometimes letting her side bangs get longer than the rest of her hair just for kicks. Like Aether, she’s considered pretty - shiny golden hair, big and alluring eyes and (mostly) delicate looking. Lumine’s legs are a different story - they’re one of her strongest (literally and figuratively) assets and she’s not shy about exploiting her so-called “Absolute Territory” for money. Her wardrobe is usually composed of either very comfortable and long hoodies and sweaters or form-fitting co-ords and dresses.
